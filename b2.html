Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer and songwriter. She is someone who possesses a narrative style of composing music, the ability to constantly transform her art, and a profound cultural influence. Swift is considered a representative figure in the popular music industry and is the focus of all public attention
Wift began pursuing the path of professional music composition at the age of 14. She signed with Big Machine Records in 2005, establishing herself as a country music artist, and she has often been referred to as the "country music princess" since then. At Big Machine Records, Swift released six studio albums and her first four studio albums were sent to country music radio, starting with her debut album Taylor Swift (2006).
